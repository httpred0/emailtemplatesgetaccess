# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Get Access marketing team members with little to no coding skills. They compose
marketing (HubSpot) and transactional (Resend) emails from pre-designed modules,
edit copy/images/links, and export HTML. They are in a production workflow, often
assembling several emails in a session.

## Product Purpose

Email Studio is an internal modular email builder for Get Access (private-market
investing platform). Emails are assembled from locked, brand-faithful modules
extracted from the Figma design system ("DS - Get Access Foundation"). Success:
any marketer ships an on-brand, client-safe email without touching code or design
tools.

## Brand Personality

Precise, quiet, luxurious. The Get Access brand (ink #161616, cream #FAF5E6, gold
#C09A6A, teal accents) shows up in the emails themselves; the tool around them
stays neutral and disappears into the task.

## Anti-references

- Marketing-landing-page styling inside the tool (heroes, display serifs, gradient text).
- Mailchimp-style visual noise; consumer-y illustration-heavy builders.
- Generic AI dashboard tells: tracked-uppercase eyebrows, glassmorphism, hero metrics.

## Design Principles

1. The canvas is the star — the tool chrome recedes, the email preview is always true-to-send.
2. Earned familiarity — affordances behave like Figma/Linear; nothing novel for novelty's sake.
3. Gold is an action, not decoration — accent only on primary actions and active state.
4. What you see is what ships — preview, thumbnails and export share one renderer.
5. Safe by construction — brand values are locked in modules; editors only touch slots.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast on its surface; reduced-motion honored; 44px touch
targets on primary controls. No WCAG audit mandate yet (internal tool), but keep
AA as the working floor.
